# Coverage

In the context of the Addon Ecosystem, the term `Coverage`  
refers to the amount of desired [Qualities] an addon processes.

<br/>

## Threshold

There are two thresholds that are used as requirements, one   
is a low threshold which is a needed for any `Indexed` addon  
a high threshold thats required for any `Curated` addon.

The high threshold represents the level of quality the FreeCAD  
project wants to be known for and is a kind of 'Seal of approval'.

<br/>

## Stability

As with anything, the list of desired [Qualities] can change  
and new items may be added or threshold may change.

When this happens, addons will be afforded a period in  
which their Affiliation / Coverage status won't change  
if the change in status is related to the updated quality.

*This will only be in effect after the addon has been reviewed.*

<br/>

## Discretion

Many of the desired quantities are either not  
quantifiable or aren't sensible to quantity like:

> The addon must address user  
> concerns [..] in a timely manner.

In cases like these it's up to the discretion of the reviewer(s) to    
decide based on the circumstances like the current efforts of  
the maintainer(s) or the history of the addon / maintainership.
